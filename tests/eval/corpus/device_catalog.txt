# Aurora Device Catalog

## Aurora Mini

颜色：银色
重量：1.2 kg
特点：便携
适用场景：移动办公

Aurora Mini is the compact member of the Aurora catalog. It is designed for people who need a lightweight device that can move between desks, meeting rooms, and travel bags. Its silver color and 1.2 kg weight distinguish it from the larger Pro and Server models.

## Aurora Pro

颜色：黑色
重量：2.1 kg
特点：高性能
适用场景：本地模型推理

Aurora Pro is the performance-oriented model. It is heavier than the Mini and Air, uses a black finish, and targets local model inference workloads where more compute is useful.

## Aurora Air

颜色：蓝色
重量：0.9 kg
特点：轻薄
适用场景：差旅

Aurora Air is the lightest model in this catalog. Its blue finish and 0.9 kg weight make it suitable for travel scenarios where portability matters more than maximum local compute.

## Aurora Server

颜色：灰色
重量：8.5 kg
特点：可扩展
适用场景：团队部署

Aurora Server is a team deployment device. It is much heavier than the portable models, uses a gray finish, and emphasizes extensibility for shared environments.
